Frequently Asked Questions

Is YugNotes free to use?

YugNotes will always offer a free tier with core features. Premium functionalities like cloud sync and AI integrations will be part of a future subscription. Detailed pricing will be announced with those features.

Which platforms will YugNotes be available on?

Initially, YugNotes is a web application accessible via any modern browser. We plan to expand to mobile (iOS/Android) and desktop platforms in subsequent updates.

How does YugNotes ensure my privacy?

We are committed to data privacy. YugNotes (currently using browser's Local Storage) employs robust encryption and security protocols for any future cloud-based services to protect your notes and personal information. Refer to our detailed Privacy Policy for more information.
