YugNotes Privacy Policy

Understanding how your data is handled with care.

Last updated: June 22, 2025

YugNotes, a product of YugVerse ("we", "us", "our"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the YugNotes application and its associated website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information, including:

  • **Personal Data:** Information you provide directly when creating an account or contacting us (e.g., email address).
  • **Usage Data:** Information about how you access and use YugNotes, such as IP address, device information, app activity, and notes metadata (e.g., creation date, last modified).
  • **Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze app usage.

Note: If YugNotes stores notes locally on device only, clarify this. If cloud sync is involved, detail data storage and encryption.

2. How Your Information Is Used

We use the collected information for purposes such as:

  • Providing and maintaining the YugNotes service.
  • Improving YugNotes features and user experience.
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries.
  • Analyzing usage to understand and enhance app performance.
  • For research and development of new features (e.g., AI-powered insights, with proper anonymization).

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not share your personal information except when:

  • You explicitly consent.
  • Required by law or legal process.
  • With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating YugNotes (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, payment processors), under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • In case of a business transfer (e.g., merger or acquisition).

4. Data Security

The security of your data is paramount to us. We implement robust security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, such as the right to access, update, delete, or restrict its processing. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • By email: support@yugverse.com
